About Us

Welcome to the Atlas Club, the premier student organization at Dolna Day School. We empower students to explore the world, expand their knowledge, and develop leadership skills. Atlas fosters creativity, curiosity, and collaboration among students.

Our Mission

We provide students with a platform to engage in meaningful activities that promote personal growth, global awareness, and a sense of community. We inspire students to think critically, act ethically, and contribute positively to society.

Our History

The Council is created on 10th May, 2024 to create a group that will work for the betterment of the school and creating a sense of community among various classes.

What We Do

The Atlas Club hosts a variety of activities, including academic competitions, cultural events, community service projects, and leadership workshops. Our events challenge members intellectually and creatively while fostering teamwork.

Join Us

If you're a student at Dolna Day School eager to learn, grow, and make a difference, we invite you to join the Atlas Club.
